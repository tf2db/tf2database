TF2 Invite Player Database --- (Season 3 to Season 19)

Hello,

I am SpaceCadet`HX and I wanted to give a brief summary of this website. Over the past several seasons, I have taken a larger interest in the history of the Invite division. Spanning from Season 3 and including the most current matches from Season 19, I have collected and recorded the history of every player that has played at least 1 match in the Invite Division.

Interested in your overall record? Map breakdown? It will be included in this website. Below are the main sections with descriptions of what can be found inside. While I have done a great deal of double-checking data, I am not perfect. Please let me know if anyone spots errors, duplicate accounts or any type of correction.
