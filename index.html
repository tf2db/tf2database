TF2 Invite player Database

Hello,

I am SpaceCadet`HX and I wanted to give a brief summary of this website. I have collected and recorded the history of every player that has played at least 1 match in the Invite Division starting from Season 3 and including the most recent matches in Season 24.

Interested in a specific overall record? Detailed Map Records? All-Time Stats for each map? It will be included in this website. Below are the main sections with descriptions of what can be found inside. While I have done a great deal of double-checking data, I am not perfect. Please let me know you spots errors, duplicate accounts or any type of correction.
Add me on Steam -- http://steamcommunity.com/id/SpaceCadetHX

I want to apologize in advance for the basic nature of this website as I am not overly smart when it comes to computer programs. Simply put, this is the best I can do. I feel the data is easy to read and navigate so hopefully that is enough.

Special thanks to Tery and several others who helped me identify duplicate records and player aliases.
